Hue

A tool to help overthinkers reframe their thought process through guided introspection.

Our perception of the world is deeply influenced by our thoughts and emotions. How we navigate and experience reality has a profound impact on how we interact with it. This project tries to help individuals prone to overthinking, by guiding them in reevaluating their thought patterns through introspection.
